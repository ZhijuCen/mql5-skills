# Trade Constants

Various constants used for programming trading strategies are divided into the following groups:

- [History Database Properties](/en/docs/constants/tradingconstants/enum_series_info_integer) – receiving general information on a symbol;

- [Order properties](/en/docs/constants/tradingconstants/orderproperties) – obtaining information about trade orders;
- [Position properties](/en/docs/constants/tradingconstants/positionproperties) – obtaining information about current positions;
- [Deal properties](/en/docs/constants/tradingconstants/dealproperties) – obtaining information about deals;
- [Trade operation types](/en/docs/constants/tradingconstants/enum_trade_request_actions) – description of trade operations available;
- [Trade transaction types](/en/docs/constants/tradingconstants/enum_trade_transaction_type) - description of possible trade transactions types;
- [Trade orders in DOM](/en/docs/constants/tradingconstants/enum_book_type) – separation of orders according to the direction of a requested operation.
